## ✨ About Us

We are Sanay and Sonay Hajirezaei, 15-year-old twin sisters from Iran, actively engaged in the fields of science, technology, and innovation. Our primary focus is on developing innovative ideas, conducting scientific research, and delivering specialized projects in the areas of artificial intelligence and programming. We are members of the International Federation of Inventors’ Associations (IFIA) and have been awarded the Silver Medal at the International Exhibition of Inventions Geneva, Switzerland, as well as the Silver Medal at the 1i1w International Competition in Istanbul. Furthermore, we were ranked among the top 44 teams worldwide in the NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025, and we were selected as one of the top 30 teams in the AIOCUP 2026 International Competition in the Netherlands. At the national level, we have also achieved several notable honors, including earning three top rankings in national artificial intelligence competitions such as Sandbox and AIOCUP, achieving 4th place nationwide in the Khwarizmi Youth Festival, being recognized among the top participants in the Student Research Center Competition, and winning 1st place at the provincial level in the “Farda” Cultural and Artistic Festival. In terms of technical expertise, our experience and skills include web design and development, artificial intelligence, financial literacy, Photoshop, computer literacy, HTML/CSS, Python, JavaScript, and PHP. These skills have enabled us to transform innovative ideas into practical, real-world, and presentable projects. We are also available for freelance collaboration on projects related to technology, web design, programming, and artificial intelligence.

### 🤖 AI Projects
- **EchoGuard AI**  
  A multimodal sentiment analysis platform that combines voice and text to detect real emotional states, useful in emergency response, support systems, and healthcare.

- **Infant Cry Analysis Platform**  
  An AI system that classifies baby cries using audio signal processing, deep learning, and language models to infer probable causes and provide caregiver guidance.

- **NLP & RAG Chatbot**  
  A specialized chatbot for analyzing NASA space biology scientific papers using Retrieval-Augmented Generation and a Streamlit interface.

- **SkyTrackNet**  
  A deep learning pipeline for bird trajectory classification from radar data using Transformer, CNN-LSTM, PyTorch, and physics-informed feature engineering.

- **Smart Camera**  
  A deep learning-based system for face recognition and emotion analysis that also detects unknown individuals and generates reports.

- **Thyroid Disease Detection**  
  A CNN and Flask-based system that analyzes thyroid PET scan images to classify patients as healthy or unhealthy.
